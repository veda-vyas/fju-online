Teaching Process

Teaching at FJU is about delivering a sequence of learning experiences design to transform a beginner into a successfully working professional who has the knowledge, skills, and behaviors to remain successful and current in their discipline.

FJU faculty members are focused on:

  1. observing students work

  2. reviewing student work products

  3. providing actionable feedback

  4. diagnosing problems blocking student progress

  5. engaging students with questions to help them think through what they are experiencing in order to find a path forward

  6. providing real-world practice to help students develop needed skills and insights as they learn knowledge of enduring value
