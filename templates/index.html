DIPLOMA PROGRAM

Mobile App Development

Mastery in Building Android Apps


Students of the diploma are required to complete 7 Android app projects in 6 - 12 months (variable duration). Student will be evaluated based on the work done on each of the 7 projects. Each project will have interim checkpoints. FJU will provide a list of open education resources in the form of MOOC, tutorials, QnA forums, etc. for the students to learn the concepts. Coach will provide an orientation for the project and will guide the student to make a project plan. This project planning would result in having a schedule for interim checkpoints for the student. Coach will use this schedule to follow up and guide the students to revise it from time to time. Student can make appointment with the coach when he/she needs help. This process goes on until the student reaching one of the interim checkpoints and at this time the coach is evaluating the work done and will provide feedback. The project plan will be revised if needed.
