General Competency Test

What is General Competency Test?

General Competency Test is a Skype based online proctored examination to evaluate your PUC level skills. This test consists of two components:

  • Aptitude Test
  • English Language Comprehension Test

Aptitude test is for 30 minutes and evaluates your quantitative, analytical, reasoning and verbal skills.
English Language comprehension Test is for 25 minutes and evaluates your capability of reading, listening, speaking and writing in English.


How do I take the Test?

This test is given through an online test portal. To take the exam you must register for the diploma program. Link to the test portal and instructions to get started will be communicated through an email at registered email address.
