We have a very simple application process

  1. Visit fju.us/apply and enter the requested information. It should take just a couple of minutes to enter the required information.

  2. Pay the application fee online The fee will vary depending on your country of residence, it will be used to cover the cost of processing your application.

  3. You will receive a confirmation email. Follow the instructions in the email to validate your email adrress, this completes your application submission process.
