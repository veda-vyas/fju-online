Student Interview & Counseling


  • FJU Admissions office will review your test results and send you an email if your scores meet the baseline requirements to enroll at FJU.

  • Please follow the instructions in the email to schedule a time for an online video interview.

  • You will need access to a computer/laptop or smartphone with Skype and a good internet connection.

  • In the interview, FJU staff will confirm your identity and go over your test results.

  • A student counselor will then talk to you about your areas of interest, you will jointly determine which FJU program or courses are right for you.

  • You will receive a confirmation email confirming your selection and details on how to register.

  • If your test scores do not meet the baseline requirements, you will be sent pointers to remedial content. You can retake the test once you review the remedial content.
