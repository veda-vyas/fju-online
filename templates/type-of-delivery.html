Type of Delivery

The faculty and students will be globally distributed and most of the founders will administer the University from the United States.


Most students will receive online delivery of educational material and will need access to a connected computing device. Every student will be assigned a designated instructor/coach at the time of registration who will have daily online interaction with the class.

A student takes courses sequentially and is able to benefit from self-paced and asynchronous nature of learning-by-doing methodology.

Many of the courses will be project centric requiring the student to work as a part a team requiring online interaction with team members.
