Teaching Assistant

As you are aware, one of the key differentiators between FJU and other online programs is the Coach who will guide you both in group sessions and one-on-one to help you understand the course material when you have questions.

If you are interested and FJU has openings, you can apply to be a Teaching Assistant to a Coach. To be eligible to apply, you need to meet the following conditions:

  • You should have taken the course you are applying to be a Teaching Assistant at FJU

  • Passed the course with 100% marks (even 99% is not acceptable) without requiring much assistance from a Coach

  • Have demonstrated excellent communication skills and work ethic


If you meet the above conditions, please contact your current Coach about applying for a part-time position as a Teaching Assistant. If accepted, you might be able to earn enough to pay up to 50% of your FJU tuition.
