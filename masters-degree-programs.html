Masters Degree

A learner with an appropriate Bachelor’s degree or equivalent may earn this credential after two full-time semesters of approved study plus a large practicum. This is typically accomplished by taking the equivalent of 8 four-unit courses plus a 16-unit practicum. This is equivalent to 3 semesters of study in most universities. All master’s degrees at FJU are professional master’s degrees aimed at accelerating and advancing the learner’s professional career, competencies and capabilities.


Master of Science in Software Development

The Master of Science in Software Development program is designed to provide an immersive learn by doing experience for students who aspire to take up roles in fast-paced software companies and tech startups. The curriculum begins with an introduction to programming, object oriented programming and databases as the foundation courses. Students then prepared to develop software for the Internet and Android devices. In addition students apply their skills and professional software development practices on real customer projects to attain a mastery in the field.
